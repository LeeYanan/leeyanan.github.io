Biography [CV]

I am currently a third year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Pheng Ann Heng. Previously, I received the B. Eng degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology in Zhejiang University in 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Deng Cai.

My research interest includes deep learning and the application in medical image computing. I am also interested in deep reinforcement learning.
